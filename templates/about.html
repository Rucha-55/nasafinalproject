About SatelliteIQ

Advanced satellite imagery analysis powered by AI for urban planning, environmental monitoring, and infrastructure development.

Our Mission

SatelliteIQ transforms complex satellite imagery into actionable insights for decision-makers across various sectors. Our platform leverages advanced deep learning algorithms to analyze land use patterns and generate predictive analytics for urban planning, environmental conservation, infrastructure development, and disaster management.

AI-Powered Analysis

State-of-the-art deep learning models trained on extensive satellite imagery datasets for accurate land use classification.

Predictive Analytics

Generate insights for urban growth, environmental impact, infrastructure needs, and disaster preparedness using AI predictions.

Professional Tools

Designed for urban planners, environmental scientists, policy makers, and researchers who need reliable spatial intelligence.

Technology Stack

Deep Learning

TensorFlow and advanced CNN architectures for image segmentation and classification

Interactive Maps

AI Predictions

Web Platform
